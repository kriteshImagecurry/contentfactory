Founded in 2014 by Sandeep Menghi,

Content Factory is a pioneering production house that seamlessly blends brand storytelling with creativity and impact. With a strong presence across India, Canada, and the Middle East, it has established itself as a leader in both audio and visual production.

At its core, Content Factory excels in delivering high-quality content at scale with economic efficiency. As consumer preferences evolve toward diverse media formats and social platforms, the company has strategically expanded by launching CF STUDIOS—a specialized division focused on high-volume, brand-driven micro-content. Leveraging cutting-edge virtual production and AI technologies, CF STUDIOS creates multilingual content tailored to clients in India, Canada, and the USA.

Beyond micro-content, Content Factory has ventured into long-form storytelling through CF CINEMA, dedicated to producing impactful medium- and long-format films that drive positive social change.

United by a shared mission to create inspiring and empowering human stories, the three entities under Content Factory harness innovation, creativity, and top-tier talent to engage global audiences. Offering flexible, tailored solutions—whether through its individual entities or as a collective force—Content Factory delivers bespoke content strategies that meet the evolving needs of brands and audiences alike.
